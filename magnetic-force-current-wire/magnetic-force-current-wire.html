Force on a Current-Carrying Wire

F = BIL sin θ  |  Direction: right-hand rule (F = IL × B)

How to use the right-hand rule: Using your right hand, point your thumb in the direction of the conventional current (I), and your fingers in the direction of the magnetic field (B). Your palm then pushes in the direction of the force (F). The magnitude depends on the angle: F = BIL sin θ — maximum at 90°, zero when parallel.

2D convention: ⊙ = vector coming out of the page (+z)   ⊗ = vector going into the page (−z).
Coordinate system: Right-handed — x̂ × ŷ = ẑ.
